Opportunity Distribution Analysis

Understanding data coverage across pairings and establishing confidence thresholds

Key Findings

  • Confidence threshold established at 7 opportunities (75th percentile) - provides stable estimates
  • Minimum threshold of 3 opportunities - pairs below this excluded from analysis due to unreliability
  • Uneven distribution is expected: Some pairings naturally work together more frequently
  • Most pairs have sufficient data: Majority of pairings exceed confidence threshold for meaningful analysis

Why Confidence Thresholds Matter

Example: A pairing with only 1 opportunity that closed won would show 100% win rate. This is statistically unreliable. By setting thresholds (3 minimum, 7 for full confidence), we ensure our recommendations are based on patterns, not random chance.
